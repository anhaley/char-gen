**Name:** Vaelin Stormarrow  
**Race:** Wood Elf  
**Class:** Fighter (Champion)  
**Ability Scores:**  
- Strength: 12  
- Dexterity: 20  
- Constitution: 14  
- Intelligence: 10  
- Wisdom: 13  
- Charisma: 8  
**Saving Throws:** Strength, Dexterity  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Acrobatics, Perception, Survival, Athletics  
**Ranged Weapon:** Longbow - Attack bonus: +11, Damage: 1d8+5  
**Melee Weapon:** Rapier - Attack bonus: +9, Damage: 1d8+5  
**Backstory:** Vaelin Stormarrow hails from the Wood Elf tribe of Skyreach. Trained in the ways of the bow and blade from a young age, Vaelin honed his skills in archery through countless hours of practice in the ancient forests of his homeland. Despite his natural talent, Vaelin is humble and always seeks to improve himself through new challenges and experiences. His ultimate goal is to become the greatest archer in the realm.  
**Racial Abilities and Features:**  
- **Darkvision:** Accustomed to twilit forests and the night sky, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. 
- **Fey Ancestry:** You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
- **Trance:** Elves don't need to sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply, remaining semiconscious, for 4 hours a day.  
- **Mask of the Wild:** You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.  
**Class Features:**  
- **Second Wind:** As a bonus action, Vaelin can regain hit points equal to 1d10 + fighter level.  
- **Action Surge:** Vaelin can push himself beyond his normal limits for a moment. On his turn, he can take an additional action on top of his regular action and a possible bonus action.  
- **Martial Archetype: Champion** - Vaelin's focus on archery has made him a master of precision and accuracy. He gains Improved Critical, allowing his weapon attacks to score a critical hit on a roll of 19-20.  
**Spell List:**  
Vaelin Stormarrow does not have access to spells as a Fighter Champion.
